Candy 4 Aliens Instructions

How To Start:

To start the game click the "Start Game" button below the game window.

Controls
Use the keyboard arrow keys to move around the game window
  • Right Arrow Key - Move your character right;
  • Left Arrow Key - Move your character Left;
  • Up Arrow Key - Use the up arrow key to jump.
Game Play

Don't get abducted by aliens, that is the goal of the game. Try to survive through invasion as long as possible. Death is unavoidable but the candy seems to kill off the aliens and prolong your survival.

Characters
Player
A child who is trying to survive an alien invasion. His only defence is his card board box and his kitchen helmet. 		Alien
Avoid them at all cost when you DON'T have "Power Up" activated from eatting candy. 		Candy
